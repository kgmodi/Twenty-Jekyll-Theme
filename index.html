Project Spark

Adventure For Charity

Project Spark is an idea where two themes adventure and charity come together. Project Spark is a journey of three people starting in New York crossing continents and driving thousands of miles from London to Mumbai for charity and adventure. As part of Project Spark, we are raising awareness and fund for two charites, Kedi School for Tribal Girls and Skanda Vale Hospice. Please visit our charities page to know more about the charities. We are partnering with Team Last Responders, a 501(c)(3) non-profit to help create awareness for our project.
