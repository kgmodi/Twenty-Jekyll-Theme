A new team member

We discussed the idea with a cousin of ours. Shrida was excited hearing about it.She lives in London and working for charity is her passion.She is a mother of an adorable two year old.Her decision was not a easy one. To leave home behind a two year old for 3 months was not an easy call.The foremost task was to convince herself.She mentioned 'I run back home at the end of work day,just to see my baby 5 minutes early'.To go without seeing her for such a long time is not painless.Her husband and parents backed her and she plunged in.

Great! More the merrier.We have a team of three people now.
