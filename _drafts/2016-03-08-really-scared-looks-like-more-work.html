March 3 2016
It's Thursday; just 2 days left for the weekly meet. I have to find visa requirements for all countries.
I have been busy at work, seeing 15-16 patients everyday. Back has been bothering. Physically exhausted. Me and Kunal are so dead by night that both try and massage each other's legs.
We were researching for bioluminescent tours in Puerto Rico
We forced woke up early and started with laptops in hands
Kunal emailed the Tibet tours today, asking if Nepal is opened up, what is the route they suggest. Thinking if we can find other cost quotations.
The most important burning topic for the day is - I found out I need letter of invitation for Kyrgyzstan visa. I don't know how to get that.
I am tensed and  anxious.
Wondering we have very little time left.
I see my CLep book on table.I haven't even opened it.
Wondering if I will be able to finish my NJ license before I leave.
Anand - the guy from overland stories. A family of 4 , had travelled from Bangalore to France. He emailed Kunal today. That was a motivating factor. We looked at his pictures and video. His little 2 nd grade 7 yr girl inspired me to continue with the journey ...
Spoke to dad today 
he asked me to organize myself more. He has always been my creative bank. He asked me to find a name for my group and said I could use full forms for abbreviations.
