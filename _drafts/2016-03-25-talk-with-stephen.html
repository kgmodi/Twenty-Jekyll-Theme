March 23
After exchanging preliminary  emails with Tata motors, we consulted Stephen if he had ideas on how to pitch the plan to Tata motors.
He suggested to have a concrete plan on the trip. We should be clear on our route, vehicle and  the required help.
He had good ideas on marketing. He suggested on the videos we can make when on the journey.

The one I loved the most was about
Asking 3 questions to all three team members
1. What are you feeling today
2. Why do you want to do the trip

These questions, put together for 3 months everyday on the trip and then clipped together as a video would be a good one to make.
