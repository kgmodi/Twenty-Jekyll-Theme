Will I be able to come back 




A very concerning factor at present is that I am on a probationary green card in United States which expires on 17th October 2016. I can file for permanent green card 3 months in advance to expiration date.
Doing it as promptly as I can,I will be able to apply on July 17 2016.Studying the online sources and a rough guideline of how long it takes for the bio metric processing,It can take anywhere from 6 weeks to months.
 It is extremely important for me and Kunal to go for the appointment for renewal.
So our original plan of starting the trip in 1st week of august  fails. And now new plan is to start last week Aug or first week September.

But here is the list of unknowns:
What IF : My process is not the same as usual and it takes longer for me to get the appointment for bio metric
What IF : What if the bio metrics fail and we are called again
What IF :  there is a delay in one of the  country during travel - means there will be a challenge in meeting the dates and deadlines for the various transit visas.

Delaying the trip more to later part of the year, means facing winter and temperature challenges to extremes.

Current plan is to be optimistic; Think positive and hope all gets done by Sep 1st and plan on.
