18 March 2016

Deciding and finalising name for the team did not look like a big task when we started with. But on deep thoughts it really looks like a task. We thought that the name of the team would reflect our purpose, our mission and our reason for the trip and hence it can not be just anything.
A few of the ideas were as follows

Team Ojas:
Ojas means vigour, energy, vitality.
The name did not appeal much to Kunal and dad though.

Team RAY - Raising awareness amongst youth.
This name was driven with the idea that we are a group of young people and doing an event which the youth would be able to connect to and relate too. We can be a small RAY of light from the entire sunshine to spread awareness, raise funds and possibly light up the life of someone.

Team ACE
The perfect abbreviation for our goal behind the trip - Adventure , Charity , Explore

Team SPARK
Spark is produced with confluence of two things and here is the confluence if two ideas of adventure and charity.
It's a spark at present which we hope to light up into a fire.
Hoping to sparkle someone's life

Project REACH ,  project Kiran were the other names we are thinking of.
Need to decide soon.

Any ideas ?
